Education

Self-taught artist with extensive training in classical and contemporary techniques

Artist Statement

My work explores the intersection of geometric precision and organic fluidity. Through abstract, geometric, pointillist, realistic, and naive styles, I seek to capture the full spectrum of human experience and natural beauty.

Techniques

  • Acrylic on Canvas
  • Oil Painting
  • Mixed Media
  • Sculpture & Installation
